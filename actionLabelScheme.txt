The current scheme for labeling driving behavior. You can think of this as a csv, where each bracketed number indicates a column. Descriptions beneath a column are mutually exclusive, while individual columns are not. For example, you can be turning left while accelerating, but you cant be turning left while turning right. Not this is the action CSV, not the measured qualities, so the speed modulation section deals with the act of changing speed, not the speed of the car itself in mph.

Parking lots and not constructed areas will be given an NA, even though parking lots vary from 5-15 and country driving is (generally) 55. since offroading and parking lot driving will likely be its own thing (if ever), this isn't much of a concern.
